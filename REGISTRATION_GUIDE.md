# Registration Guide

## How to Register

### For Students:
1. Click the ðŸ‘¤ emoji in the top right
2. Click the **Register** tab
3. Fill in:
   - **Full name**: Your name
   - **Email**: Any email address (no restrictions)
   - **Password**: Must have:
     - At least 8 characters
     - 1 uppercase letter (A-Z)
     - 1 lowercase letter (a-z)
     - 1 special character (!@#$%^&* etc)
   - Keep **"Student"** selected (default)
4. Click **Create Account**

### For Admins:
1. Click the ðŸ‘¤ emoji in the top right
2. Click the **Register** tab
3. Fill in all fields (same as above)
4. **Select "Admin (manage trash cans)"** instead of Student
5. Click **Create Account**
6. You'll now have access to the **Admin** tab where you can:
   - Create new trash cans
   - Upload images for trash cans
   - View all trash cans
   - Download QR codes

## Login
1. Click the ðŸ‘¤ emoji

4. Click **Sign In**
